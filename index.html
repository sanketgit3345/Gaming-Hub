Tic Tak Toe

Tic Tac Toe, also known as Noughts and Crosses, is a classic paper-and-pencil game typically played on a 3x3 grid. The game involves two players, one using "X" markers and the other using "O" markers, taking turns to place their respective symbols in empty cells of the grid. The objective of the game is to form a straight line of three of one's own markers horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.!
